2048

How to Play

2048 is an addictive puzzle game where your goal is to combine matching numbers by sliding them in any of the four directions (up, down, left, right). When two tiles with the same number collide, they merge into one tile with the sum of their values. Keep combining numbers until you create the 2048 tile! The game ends when you can no longer make any moves. Use your arrow keys or swipe on mobile to move the tiles.
