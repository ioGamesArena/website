Tiny Fishing

How to Play

Tiny Fishing is a relaxing fishing game where you cast your line into the water and try to catch as many fish as possible. Click or tap to cast your line, then wait for a fish to bite. When a fish takes the bait, quickly reel it in! Earn money from your catches to upgrade your fishing rod, line strength, and other equipment. The better your gear, the bigger the fish you can catch. Discover different species of fish and try to catch them all while enjoying the peaceful fishing experience.
