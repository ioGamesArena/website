Farming Island

If you’ve ever zoned out at work, dreaming about quitting everything and running your own farm on a sunny island… yeah, same. Farming Island lets you do just that, without ever leaving the city or even your browser. Farming Island Online is a relaxed, clicker-style farming game where you explore a peaceful island, plant crops, raise animals, fish a little, and stack up some digital coins. You start off with a nice little plot of land, a few thousand coins in your pocket, and no clue what you’re doing, but that’s part of the fun.
