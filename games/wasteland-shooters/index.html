Wasteland Shooters

Wasteland Shooters is a game where you make your own way to the journey of shooters. Your mission is to keep the coordination right and be vigilant as you shoot the enemies. Each area in the game gives the opportunity to find spaces to hide, hunt and attack the opponents. The weapons will help you reach the best player in the shooter world.
