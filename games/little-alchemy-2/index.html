Little Alchemy 2

How to Play

Little Alchemy 2 is a crafting game where you start with four basic elements: air, earth, fire, and water. Combine these elements by dragging and dropping them onto each other to create new items. For example, combining water and earth creates mud, while fire and earth makes lava. With over 700 items to discover, let your creativity and logic guide you to unlock new combinations and find all the possible elements in this enchanting puzzle game!
