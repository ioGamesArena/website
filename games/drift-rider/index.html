Drift Rider

Drift Rider is a 3D drift game where the players can customize their vehicle and then hit the road. There are 5 distinct racetracks that the player can drift on. You may drive on a highway or in the streets of a city. Or, take the task of navigating a curving and narrow intercity route by drifting.
