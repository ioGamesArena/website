Mech Shooter

Mech Shooter is a 3D robot shooting game where you, in the shoes of Humanoid Mech, will deal with many robots trying to destroy you and your team. The game map can be a factory or somewhere in the city. No matter where you are, you will be with your teammates. Use your Minigun and eliminate all enemy forces in one of the most thrilling action games on Online Games!
