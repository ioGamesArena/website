Tunnel Rush

How to Play

Tunnel Rush is an intense 3D action game where you speed through a geometric tunnel filled with colorful obstacles. Use the left and right arrow keys (or A and D) to rotate the tunnel and avoid crashing into the barriers. As you progress, the speed increases and the patterns become more complex, testing your reflexes and concentration. The vibrant visuals and pulsing soundtrack create an immersive experience that will keep you coming back for more. How far can you make it through the endless tunnel?
