Super Mini Racing

A super duper race is about to begin in just a few minutes! Choose your car and take your position behind the wheel for Super Mini Racing—a 3D racing game that offers both single-player and two-player modes. You'll be driving super mini cars across 10 different racetracks, competing to be the winner and claim the prize. Controls Single Player or Player 1 Steer: WASD keys Respawn: R Player 2 Steer: Arrow keys Respawn: M How to Play Super Mini Racing Select either single-player or 2-player mode.
