Cake Match Puzzle

Cake Match Puzzle is an addictive free game where you become a bakery chef. You’ll play with cake slices on a 4x4 tray, merging them to create full cakes. The goal is simple: make as many cakes as possible before you run out of moves. It’s all about strategy, smart placement, and a little bit of luck!
