Speedrun Parkour

Alex, a young boy with great athletic abilities, finds himself in a port. He is chased by the bad guys of the port. His only way out is to navigate a series of heart-pounding challenges, leaping, rolling, and climbing over containers, boxes, and various other objects scattered in the sky. Your mission is to guide him to safety.
